History of Photography

The first camera was created in the late 1930s by Joseph Nicéphore Niépce in france. The first ever photo to about 8 hours to shoot. many people had to pose for a long time for the photo to finish, this resulted in the people not smiling or even the photographer used dead bodies as props. In the early 1940's the polaroid camera was invented by Edwin H. Land. During the 1970's the cameras like polaroid were becoming popular due to them being instant film cameras.
