Photography Website

Photography is an art form used by a camera. It can be either digital or film. There are many different types of cameras from Canon, nikon, panasonic. If you go the old film route you will need to develop the photos, but with digital you already have the photo saved on a storage device. It is easer to edit digital photos with photoshop. There are also many editing applications you can use to manipulate your pictures.
